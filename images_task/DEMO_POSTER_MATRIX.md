# DEMO POSTER MATRIX

Global rules for all posters:

- PNG.
- Vertical 2:3 poster.
- Professional real-poster look.
- Not minimal.
- Not mock.
- Not placeholder.
- Not icon-only.
- Not abstract-only.
- Large readable event title.
- Human figure or group of people required as the main visual anchor.
- Every poster must have its own genre-specific composition.
- No repeated border/frame/grid/template across the set.
- No real logos or recognizable real celebrities.
