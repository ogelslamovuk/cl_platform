# Review protocol

## Why test-first

The previous poster attempt failed because the result looked like one template recolored several times. To avoid repeating that, the first step is a single test poster.

## Test poster

First generate only:

```text
poster-mikhail-stasov-vernus-k-tebe.png
```

This poster is the most important visual anchor of the demo афиша. It must establish the quality bar for the whole set.

## What to check on the test poster

- Does it look like a real commercial concert poster?
- Does it feel like a major star concert, not a mock?
- Is the human figure the main visual anchor?
- Is the title readable in a small event card?
- Does it avoid minimal/icon/placeholder style?
- Does it avoid resemblance to a real celebrity?
- Is the composition rich enough for a premium B2C афиша?

## After approval

Generate all remaining posters according to the matrix, preserving the quality level but changing genre, composition, color and mood for every event.
